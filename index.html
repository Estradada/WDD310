Colombia

The Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta is a majestic mountain range located in the northern region of Colombia, South America. It is a unique mountain system that rises sharply from the warm beaches of the Caribbean Sea to extraordinary altitudes, creating impressive ecological diversity in a relatively small geographic space. Known for its cultural richness, biodiversity, and unique geographical features, the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta is home to diverse indigenous communities, including the Koguis, Arhuacos, Wiwas, and Kankuamos.
